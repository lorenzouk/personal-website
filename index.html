Automatic Tilemapping

TypeScript

During my summer research program, my team and I worked on developing an automatic tilemapping system aimed at answering the question: How can we generate natural language descriptions for each infividual tile within an arbitrary tilemap with minimal user input? While we weren't able to achieve a perfect result, our system made progress twoard reducing the amount of input required from the user.

I built a BFS apporach to match tiles based on the tile description and worked on the implementation of the LLM and model.

Zero Down

PC | JavaScript, Phaser, Tiled

Zero Down is a top-down gallery shooter set during the Battle of Midway. The player pilots an American aircraft tasked with shooting down waves of incoming Japanese planes while dodging enemy fire. I designed and implemented core gameplay systems, including smooth player controls, bullet mechanics, and enemy behavior. The game features enemy planes capable of tracking the player’s position and difficulty scaling to keep the action challenging and engaging.
